Instructions


1. The first person to get to 200 points wins! You can change the target score by typing a number into the 'Final Score' box.


2. By pressing 'Roll Dice,' you will roll two dice. If you are happy with your score, you will press 'Hold,' this will end your turn and add your points to your total score. If you want to try and go for more points, you can continue rolling by pressing 'Roll Dice.'


3. If either of your dice are a 1, your turn will end and you will receive no points for that round.


4. If both dice are a 1, your total score will reset.


5. If both dice are equal to six, you will receive double points for that roll.


Good luck!
